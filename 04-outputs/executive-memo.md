**Salesforce License Right-Sizing — $561K annual savings.** The target company is spending $3.1M annually on Salesforce, almost certainly carrying 20-30% license bloat typical of enterprise customers. This is low-effort work with the highest dollar impact. Sales leadership will need assurance that active user capabilities remain unchanged. Timeline: 60 days post-close to audit, 90 days to renewal negotiation.

**Professional Services Consolidation — $111K annual savings.** Five separate vendors provide accounting advisory and recruitment services without volume leverage. Consolidating BDO and four recruitment agencies onto two preferred partners with master service agreements will reduce contingency recruiting fees from 20-25% to 15-18% and lock in advisory retainer pricing. We preserve tactical flexibility for specialized roles (Salesforce recruiting) within the MSA structure. Timeline: 90 days post-close.
